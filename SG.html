Stein's Gate is a visual novel developed by 5pb and Nitroplus studios. The story of Stein's Gate begins in Akihabara on July 28, 2010 and tells about four friends who created a machine that allows you to send letters to the past, from a microwave oven. By conducting various experiments, they attract the attention of the SERN organization, which is engaged in the study of time travel.
