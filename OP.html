The ninja-pirate-minko-Samurai alliance puts into action its plan to attract allies to the country of Vano in order to free it from the oppression of Shogun Kurozumi Orochi and his allies, the Beast Pirates led by the Kaido Clan. Meanwhile, the pirates of Big Mama are also heading to Vano to take revenge on the Mugiwars for what they had previously staged in Toto Land.
