In the center of the story is a high school student Shigeo Kageyame, nicknamed Mob, which he received for his inconspicuousness. But at the same time he is a powerful psychic. The Mob itself considers its abilities dangerous and tries to restrain emotions in order to maintain control over itself. He's trying to live the life of an ordinary person. That's just his suppressed emotions and psychic abilities periodically still break out.
